Power Generation Machines

Power generation machines are devices or systems that convert various forms of energy into electrical power.
Electricity is a crucial form of energy that is used extensively in our daily lives and various industries.
Power generation machines come in different types, each harnessing a specific energy source to produce electricity.

Internal Combustion Engines

Internal Combustion Engines (ICEs) are devices that convert the chemical energy of fuel into mechanical energy through combustion (burning) within the engine. These engines are commonly used in vehicles, such as cars, trucks, motorcycles, and aircraft, as well as in various industrial and power generation applications.
The most common types of internal combustion engines are gasoline engines (spark-ignition engines) and diesel engines (compression-ignition engines).

Machine Tools

Machine tools are devices or machines used to shape, cut, and form metal or other materials into desired shapes and sizes.
They are essential in manufacturing processes and play a crucial role in producing various products.

Material Handling Machines

Material handling machines are specialized equipment designed to move, lift, transport, and manipulate materials and goods within a manufacturing, warehouse, construction, or logistics environment.
These machines improve efficiency, reduce manual labor, and enhance safety in material handling operations.

Pumps and Compressor

Pumps and compressors are both types of fluid handling machines used to move and manipulate gases or liquids.
While they serve similar purposes in some cases, their fundamental principles and applications differ.
